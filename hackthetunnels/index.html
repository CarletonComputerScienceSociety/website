What is Hack The Tunnels?




Hack The Tunnels is a "Challenge" based Hackathon.

Participants will work in teams to build various features for a mystery project. By completing these features, participants will learn various web development and software engineering concepts while earning points that will count towards winning prizes. Teams will hack with the languages, libraries, and frameworks of their choosing. However, a project template will also be provided.

The event will take place over two days. Day one will be decicated to teaching core concepts neccesary to complete challenges on day two. We recommend participants who have not taken COMP 2406 attend Day one. Day two is the main event where participants will complete various challenges, attend workshops, and win prizes!
