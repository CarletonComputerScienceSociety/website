CCSS Elections 2021

Written By: William So

The general election period for the CCSS Board of Directors of the 2021-2022 term begins today!

February 26, 2021

There are six positions to run for:

  • President
  • Vice President
  • Director of Finance
  • Director of Academics
  • Director of Social
  • Director of Community

You can find the full election timeline and further details on the electoral policies outlined in our Writ of Election.

Interested in running for a position? Fill out the nomination form by Wednesday, March 3.

Please contact elections@ccss.carleton.ca with any questions or concerns regarding the CCSS elections.
